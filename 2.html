Kick It

"Kick It" (Korean: 영웅; Hanja: 英雄; RR: Yeong-ung; MR: Yǒngung; lit. Hero) is a song recorded by South Korean boy band NCT 127, the multi-national and Seoul-based unit of the unlimited boy group NCT under the management of SM Entertainment.Described as a "high-energy" song with "inspirational lyrics" that portrays the group's musical identity, it was released as the lead single from their second studio album Neo Zone on March 6, 2020.
